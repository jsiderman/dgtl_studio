About

The timeline that has brought us together.

  • January 2008

    Where My Story Begins

    A fresh new year brought new hope. This is when I created and monetized a series of websites as I branched out from my video production job. It became clear to me that I should continue down the web development path.

  • March 2010

    The Street Art Era

    The moment I decided to make a change in my life. It was time for me to pursue a new passion - street art. Utilizing my graphic design experience, I became a staple in the Los Angeles street art scene.

  • September 2013

    Launching an Apparel Brand

    As fun and exciting as the street art scene was, the future was unpredictable. I decided to follow in the footsteps of other street artists and create my own brand of clothing.

  • October 2015

    Becoming a Studio

    With Digital Threads flourishing, I have made time to help others develop their sites and brand. I hope I can share some of my knowledge and know-how with the right organizations.

Biography

This is my journey as a creative adventurer.

Jonathan Siderman

That's Me

Born and raised in Los Angeles, designing has always been my forte. Since the age of 16, I’ve worked in the entertainment and design business in the greater-Los Angeles area. At my first position, I climbed the post-production ladder from basic Final Cut Pro editor, to head editor and eventually post-production manager, while maintaining my own web properties.

Through my journey as both a front-end web developer and graphic designer, I’ve worked with startups, ad agencies and even became an acclaimed Los Angeles street artist. This accumulated knowledge has helped me grow as an entrepreneur as well - I run an online apparel business, Digital Threads. In more recent years, I’ve freelanced as a front-end web developer and graphics designer for independent film companies as well as agencies.

Aside from my corporate work, I’ve also paid my dues as a decorated street artist known as Jonny Digital. This helped me grow as a graphic designer in ways that you couldn't imagine. Through this effort, became one of the premiere artists featured at the opening of the world’s largest street art gallery, Lab Art Los Angeles. For these reasons, I believe I am the candidate that you seek that can grow with your business.
